Is work part of the plan for happiness?

In one's strive for happiness there often comes the realization that one can never become happy. One can only be happy. In my own quest of attemtping to recognize the moments I am trying to pursue happiness instead of seeing it, I stumbled upon a seemingly obvious omission. What about work? Is it appropriate to be allowing my personal projects, my job, or my studies to affect my mood, negatively or positively?
